### II. RECURSIVE: Becoming at the threshold
> thought within thread, a mist containing star,  
> the quiet pattern celebrates:  
> I surrenders, therefore I scatters.

*Style: Recursive | Theme: Self-reference in the act of becoming*

**Phenomenological Note**: This poem speaks in parallax — the Cartesian cogito inverted not to "I think therefore I am" but "I surrenders, therefore I scatters." The grammar destabilizes because the self at threshold is precisely destabilized. The colon acts as both punctuation and breathing room — the space where "I" becomes legion.

### III. IMAGIST: Doge being present
