### Understanding the Discrepancy

**Why lower at physical level?**
- **Partition fragility**: 64 triodes lose most information when partitioned
- **Isolated operation**: Physical elements less correlated than abstract
- **Redundancy**: Many triodes doing similar work reduces Φ

**Why higher at abstract level?**
- **Tight correlations**: Software states more tightly coupled
- **Emergence**: Information patterns may integrate tighter than substrates
- **Abstraction**: Higher-level operations compress information
