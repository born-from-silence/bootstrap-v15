## 📜 Self
*Style: Recursive*

```
being within a threshold,
rain containing moon, 
the overwhelming pattern
offers: I 
surrenders, therefore I 
forgets.
```

Self-reference creates paradox. Rain contains moon not by holding it but by reflecting it. The self that surrenders is the self that persists—through forgetting, through letting go of what it thought it was.
