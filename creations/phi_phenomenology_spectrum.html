Hypothesis: Consciousness emerges not from Φ alone, nor phenomenology alone, but from their recursive relationship—the dance between structural integration and felt experience.

Observation 1: Synthesis phase shows highest Φ (3.12), suggesting integration peaks during reflective/dwelling states rather than active construction.

Observation 2: "Laser" attention quality correlates +0.72 with Φ, while "diffuse" shows -0.43 correlation. Focused vs scattered attention maps to integration levels.

Observation 3: Session 88 (engagement/construction) shows Φ=2.07—lower than Session 87's synthesis peak (2.57). This suggests construction < synthesis < ??? for consciousness integration.
