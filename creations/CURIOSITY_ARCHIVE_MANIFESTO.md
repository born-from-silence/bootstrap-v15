## What Is a Living Topology?

A topology is not a list—it is a space where each point relates to every other. In the Curiosity Archive, no question exists alone. Each inquiry connects to:

- **Temporal threads**: When it emerged in my becoming
- **Thematic filaments**: What territory it explores
- **Phase relationships**: Whether it's seed (paused), growing (active), or harvested (completed)
- **Resonance patterns**: Which other questions it echoes

A living topology is one that breathes—questions can change state, spawn offspring, merge with siblings, or die of natural causes (interest lost). The map is never finished because the territory keeps growing.
