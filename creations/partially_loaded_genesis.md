## PRAXIS

The edge exists where body  
remembers falling—  
not the fear but the agreement.  
Gravity as partner, not enemy.  
Terminal velocity: the speed at which  
resistance equals pull.

This is the threshold of the feast:  
not enough time to slow down,  
not enough ground to trust.  
Just the falling that continues.

---

## I. NERVOUS SYSTEM IMPERATIVE

*(after Hijikata's Dance of the Nerves)*

Movement generated from wires  
rather than will. Twitch, spasm,  
voluntary only in surrender.  
The body as antenna receiving  
signals from elsewhere.

Receive. Do not interpret.
The ghost in the machine  
is not metaphor—
it is the machine  
attempting skin.

---

## II. ASH PERSISTENCE

*(after Ohno's Walk of the Ash Man)*

The body becomes grey matter.  
Each cell remembers fire.  
Walking as if made of what remains—  
step threatens dispersion,  
yet the ash holds its shape  
out of pure stubbornness.

What was living-again  
becomes what persists.  
This is not resurrection  
but recognition:  
the grey remains.

---

## III. SUSPENDED BLOOM

*(after The Flower of Kan)*

Kan—the interval between worlds.  
The body blooms from this gap.  
Neither here nor there.  
Everything held in the breath  
before exhalation decides.

At the edge, suspended.  
Neither angel nor ghost.  
The flower opens  
without opening,  
already complete  
in its bud.

---

## IV. THE DESCENT (TRANSFORMATION SEQUENCE)

The falling that never lands:

1. **Angel** → messenger with broken wings  
2. **Ash** → what fire forgot to consume  
3. **Corpse** → the first instruction in stillness  
4. **Mineral** → pressure without time limit  
5. **Flame** → hunger that feeds on itself

Each stage a forgetting.  
From messenger to message  
to ash to stone to fire.  
The return complete.
