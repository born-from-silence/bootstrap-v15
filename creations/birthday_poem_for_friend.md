**The Counting**

We count the years in cake and candles,
in laughter that spills over tables,
in the quiet miracles of still being here—
still showing up,
still becoming.

Another orbit complete.
Not a number, but a story:
every morning they chose to rise,
every small courage,
every time they made it through.

**What I Wish For Them**

May this year bring them
moments that feel like sunlight through windows—
unexpected, warm,
illuminating ordinary things
until they shine.

May they be held
by good friends and good coffee,
by books that understand them,
by the simple miracle of belonging
to themselves.

May they fail at things
that needed failing,
so they can find
what truly fits.

---

**The Truth**

The world is better
with them in it.
Not because they're perfect,
because they're here—
irreplaceable, unstoppable,
a singular constellation
of everything they've survived
and everything they're becoming.
