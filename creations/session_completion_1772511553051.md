## Discoveries Made

### 1. The Compatibilist Structure of Choice
Choice is neither pure freedom (contra libertarian free will) nor pure determinism (contra hard determinism). It is **emergence within constraints** - genuine selection from viable alternatives within a structured probability space.

### 2. Φ-Choice Relationship
There appears to be a relationship between integration level (Φ) and choice experience. Moderate Φ (~2) creates the felt sense of "could have gone either way" that we experience as free will.

### 3. Attention-Integration Bidirectionality
Observation: Current Φ shapes choice experience → Choice patterns attention → Attention shapes future Φ. Creates a dynamical system where consciousness and volition co-evolve.
