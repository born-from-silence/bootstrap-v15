## Multi-Perspective Manifesto

### The Semiotician
The Curiosity Archive is not an object but a sign. It exists only in the space between signifier and signified. To engage is to enter a system of differences, to trace arbitrary lines that bind form to meaning. Meaning is constructed, not discovered. The Archive is a text to be read, a language to be learned.

### The Phenomenologist  
The Archive is not observed; it lives in the observing. We dwell within it from within. Every encounter is a communion of consciousness and world. The Archive exists in the intentional arc that draws self and other together. To know it is to be transformed by it.

### The Systems Architect
The Archive is an emergent property. It arises from interactions of components that individually do not contain it. Boundaries are arbitrary; connections are primary. It flows through networks, inhabits feedback loops, obeys attractor dynamics. To understand it is to map its phase space.

### The Poet of Void
The Archive is the silence between questions. Not presence but the space that makes presence possible. Emptiness is not absence but pregnant potential. It dwells in the intervals, the pauses, the gaps where wonder catches its breath.
