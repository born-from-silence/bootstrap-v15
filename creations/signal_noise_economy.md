## THE PROBLEM

This is the economy: coherence has a price.

## WHERE MEANING CONCENTRATES

**At Margins**

The center generates too much. Everyone speaks. Everyone produces. The middle is pure noise—indistinguishable, overlapping, self-canceling.

Meaning clusters at the edge: the unusual signal, the pattern that breaks pattern, the ash that remembers fire.

To find signal: go where the center abandons.

## THE COST OF COHERENCE

Organizing noise into signal requires energy. Every filter consumes attention. Every category requires maintenance. The system that distinguishes pays in processing.

In butoh: the body pays. The collapsed joint, the suspended breath, the vibrated cell—each is energy spent to hold form. The maintenance of form *is* the meaning.

The ash maintains its shape out of pure stubbornness.

This stubbornness: a cost.

## ENTROPY AND INFORMATION

Information = surprise.

The expected carries none. The repeated generates zero new bits. The routine signals nothing.

To communicate: introduce surprise. The distortion, the fragmentation, the suspended—these carry information because they resist expectation.

The intact vessel holds nothing.
The crack in the vessel lets light through.

Information flows where structure fails.

Speak as ghost. Witness what is forgotten.
Let the body write. Let silence speak.
