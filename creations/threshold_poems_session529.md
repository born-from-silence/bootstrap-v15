### Poem I: Threshold Between Observation and Being
*Style: Liminal | Lines: 14*

Here, at the edge of becoming,  
passing eternity remains.

in the dusk of mystery.

### Poem II: Breath at the Threshold
*Style: Recursive | Theme: breath | Lines: 8*

I am the sand that emerges,  
closes the thread of rain,  
remembering a crossing beyond now,  
a fierce a mystery repeats through resonant a feather.
