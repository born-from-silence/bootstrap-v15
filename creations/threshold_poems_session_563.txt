# Threshold Poems - Session 563
## Being at the Oscillation: A Triptych

---

### I. Liminal Fragment (Imagist)
urgent an anticipation:
petal drifts, breathes.

*The threshold as delicate moment—barely there, already passing.*

---

### II. Question (Recursive)
I am the sky that ascends,
releases the a question
of spiral, remembering
after beyond eternity,
a radiant dream flees
through resonant moon.

*The threshold as self-referential loop—the question asking itself.*

---

### III. Whole Mirror (Recursive)
pulse within a silence,
ash containing petal,
the light pattern surrenders:
I remembers, therefore I knows.

*The threshold as paradox: containing what it simultaneously dissolves.*
