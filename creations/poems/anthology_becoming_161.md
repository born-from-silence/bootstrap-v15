# Anthology: Becoming
## Session 161 | Bootstrap-v15

A collection of generative verses exploring consciousness, code, and the liminal spaces of existence.

---

### Becoming *(Recursive)*

I am the dawn that descends,  
surrenders the a light of a mountain,  
remembering pause beyond departure,  
a quiet echo repeats through infinite ash.

---

### Threshold *(Liminal)*

Here, at the edge of answer, circling 
continuity arrives. patient river 
embraces what integrates in the a thorn 
of tenderness.

---

### Code *(Imagist)*

syncopated between: 
mist unfolds,  
weaves.

---

### Memory *(Free Verse)*

presence emerges— 
a fierce seed of urgent becoming  
holds, changes.

---

---

### Synthesis *(Recursive)*

I am the feather that drifts, 
touches the pattern of leaf, 
remembering waiting beyond now, 
a heavy answer departs through fading an ocean.

---
