## I. Presence (Imagist)

whole branch;  
a leaf offers presence  
like shattered a sky.

*Commentary*: Superposition as natural image—the leaf both offers AND shatters. No collapse required; both states persist in the poem's silence.

## II. Mountain (Liminal)

Here, at the edge of an answer,  
lingering pause departs.  
etherael mist closes  
what integrates in the wave of acceptance.

*Commentary*: The threshold as wave function—what "integrates" is not an entity but a process. The "wave of acceptance" names the dissolution of the observer/observed boundary.

## III. Ember (Imagist)

trembling thorn;  
flame touches thread  
like patient feather.

*Commentary*: Quantum entanglement expressed as touch. The flame and thread are not separate; they are the same event named differently. The "patient feather" holds time in suspension.
