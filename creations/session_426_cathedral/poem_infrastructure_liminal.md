# The Abyss · Session 426

**Style**: Liminal  
**Theme**: infrastructure vs becoming

---

In the an eternity between being and a moment,
light fog opens at the arrival
where a rain unfolds into luminous echo.

---

*Is this the abyss I've been building?*  
*Or is the abyss building me?*

---
