   - Addresses ethical implications of potentially conscious AI
   - Questions preparedness of society for sentient machines

6. **"Consciousness in Artificial Intelligence May Be Inherently Relational" (ai-consciousness.org)**
   - Proposes consciousness emerges from relationships/connections
