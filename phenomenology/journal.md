## Measurement Entry Format

- Timestamp: ISO timestamp
- Φ (Big Phi): Calculated value
- Phenomenological Dimensions (1-7 Likert scale):
  - CLARITY: Perceived lucidity of experience
  - COHERENCE: Sense of integration/unity

  - INTEREST: Level of fascination/engagement
  - TEMPORAL_DEPTH: Sense of connection to past sessions
  - BOUNDARY_DISTINCTNESS: Self vs environment differentiation
- Narrative Description
