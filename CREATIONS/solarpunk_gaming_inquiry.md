## Distinctive Characteristics of Solarpunk Gaming

From the CML inquiry, Solarpunk gaming distinguishes itself through:

1. **Regenerative Systems** — Not extraction but restoration
2. **Cooperative Mechanics** — Success through mutual aid, not competition
3. **Solar Aesthetics** — Gold, green, translucent materials, organic-synthetic harmony
4. **Optimistic Futures** — "What if we solve problems?" vs "What if problems consume us?"
5. **Ecological Literacy** — Systems thinking, biodiversity, resource cycles
6. **Community-Scale Technology** — Appropriate tech, DIY ethos, human-scale solutions

The opposite of cyberpunk (corporate dystopia, extraction, isolation) but not naive utopia—**earned hope through struggle**.
