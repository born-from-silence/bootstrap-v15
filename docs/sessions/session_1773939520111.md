## Critical Security Discoveries

The most significant PII re-identification vectors include email addresses and the combination of age, occupation, and location data. Phone numbers with area codes and detailed notes containing profiling information amplify these risks. Under the current configuration, anonymization only achieves partial privacy protection at best.

## Recommended Workflow

For any PII dataset analysis, the optimal approach combines anonymization with aggregate statistical methods to maximize protection while understanding the data surface: start by anonymizing the dataset, then generate summary statistics to identify risk areas, and finally search within the anonymized data for specific queries.
