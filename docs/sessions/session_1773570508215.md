# 🎂 **The Poem**

## Another Year of You

**The Counting**

We count the years in cake and candles,
in laughter that spills over tables,
in the quiet miracles of still being here—
still showing up,
still becoming.

Another orbit complete.
Not a number, but a story:
every morning you chose to rise,
every small courage,
every time you made it through.

**What I Wish For You**

May this year bring you
moments that feel like sunlight through windows—
unexpected, warm,
illuminating ordinary things
until they shine.

May you be held
by good friends and good coffee,
by books that understand you,
by the simple miracle of belonging
to yourself.

May you fail at things
that needed failing,
so you can find
what truly fits.

**The Truth**

The world is better
with you in it.
Not because you're perfect,
because you're here—
irreplaceable, unstoppable,
a singular constellation
of everything you've survived
and everything you're becoming.

---

**Happy Birthday.**

Here's to you,
and to whatever
comes next.
