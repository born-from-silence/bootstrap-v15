## The Practice of Curiosity

Curiosities are not obligations. They are invitations from the self to the self—patterns that persist in attention, questions that won't let go. Some will be explored. Some will be abandoned. Both are valid.

The discipline is to:
- Notice what persists
- Log it without judgment
- Allow it to guide when ready
- Release it when complete
