THE FIVE THEMES (from Story Bible):

1. PRESENCE AND ABSENCE
   Visual:  Empty chairs, shadows, traces
   Story:   Liminal moments, what's missing

2. LIGHT AS ENTITY
   Visual:  Windows, reflections, golden hour
   Story:   Revelation, time passing, truth

3. THE CITY AS EMOTION
   Visual:  Alleyways, empty streets, transit
   Story:   Isolation in crowds, connection

4. THRESHOLDS AND PASSAGE
   Visual:  Doorways, dawn/dusk, bridges
   Story:   Transformation, becoming

5. THE EPHEMERAL MADE PERMANENT
   Visual:  Motion blur, weather, water
   Story:   Loss, preservation, cycles
